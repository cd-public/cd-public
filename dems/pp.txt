FUNDING PLANNED PARENTHOOD

(Orange)

WHEREAS, Planned Parenthood provides vital services

in healthcare that are provided by no other 

organization; and

WHEREAS, Planned Parenthood enjoys a higher 

approval rating than the US Congress^1;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party supports funding Planned Parenthood.
