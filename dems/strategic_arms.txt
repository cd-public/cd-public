REDUCTION OF STRATEGIC ARMS

(Orange)

WHEREAS, strategic arms pose an unparalleled 

existential threat to the long-term security 

of all human life; and

WHEREAS, approximately $20 billion per year^1 are 

spent in maintence of a large and diverse nuclear

arsenal; and

WHEREAS, the rise of non-state actors has further

increased the risk posed by maintaining nuclear

arsenals; and

WHEREAS, the Bulletin of Atomic Scientists find

threat of crisis higher today that at any time

since the height of the cold war^2;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party supports reduction of strategic arms.
