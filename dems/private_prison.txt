END PRIVATE PRISONS

(Orange)

WHEREAS, the United States Justice Department

should ensure consistency in safety, security 

and rehabilitation services by operating its 

own prison facilities^1; 

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party seeks to end the use of private prisons.
