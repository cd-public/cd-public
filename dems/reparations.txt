REPARATIONS

(Orange County)

WHEREAS, the United States has a long and troubled

history of mistreating Americans of African descent,

and

WHEREAS, the only credible path to address this ill

is reparations^123, beginning with the passage of 

the "Commission to Study Reparation Proposals for 

African-Americans Act", 

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party supports reparations.
