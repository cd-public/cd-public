RECLASSIFY DRUG USE AS A PUBLIC HEALTH ISSUE

(Orange)

WHEREAS, many regions of America have been ravaged by

opiod abuse epidemics; and

WHEREAS, many developed nations have seen improved public

health outcomes by treating drug use as a public health

rather than criminal matter;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party supports reclassifying drug use a public health 

issue.
