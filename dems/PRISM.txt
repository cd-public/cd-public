CESSATION OF PRISM PROGRAM

(Orange)

WHEREAS, United States security hinges fundamental

on strong relationships with our allies; and

WHEREAS, relationships wither when trust is breached;

and

WHEREAS, the Constitutions Fourth Amendment protects

Americans from unreasonable searches; and

WHEREAS, the PRISM program^1 violates both 

Fourth Amendment protections and our trust

with our allies;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party supports the cessation of PRISM programs.
