REGULATING CANNABIS AS ALCOHOL

(Orange)

WHEREAS, scientific evidence now suggests that cannabis

is safer than alcohol and tobacco; and

WHEREAS, the State of Colorado has seen enormous public

benefits from regulating cannabis like alcohol^1; and

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party regulating cannabis as alcohol.
