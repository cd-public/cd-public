OPPOSE PROTECTION OF LAWFUL COMMERCE IN ARMS ACT

(Orange)

WHEREAS, the United States is experiencing and

epidemic of mass shootings; and

WHEREAS, the Protection of Lawful Commerce in

Arms Act protects corporations from being held

accountable for safely distributing their firearms;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party opposes the Protection of Lawful Commerce

in Arms Act.
