NONDISCRIMINATION LAW

(Orange)

WHEREAS, House Bill 2 has greatly diminished the

esteem with which North Carolina is regarded; and

WHEREAS, repealing is insufficient to protect

Americans of all backgrounds; and

WHEREAS, discrimination on basis of race, creed,

age, sex. gender identity or expression, sexuality

or any other basis in any context, be it employment,

government services, or anything else is in direct 

opposition to the most basic tennents of the 

Constitution;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,

that the 4th Congressional District Democratic

Party supports passage of state- and federal-level

non-discrimination law.
